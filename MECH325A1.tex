	\subsection{Power Screw Analysis}
	
	The objective of this section is to find the minimum required torque and rotational speed needed to lift the 2500kg load at 4 mm/sec.
	
	The torque required to lift a load with gravitational force $F$ is:
	
	\begin{equation}
	\tau = \frac{Fd_m}{2}\left(\frac{l+\pi f d_m}{\pi d_m - fl}\right)
	\end{equation}
